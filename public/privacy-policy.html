Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

DeMar Logistics LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the DeMar Logistics mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

When you use the DeMar Logistics App, we may collect the following information:

  • Phone Number: Used for driver identification and account verification via magic link authentication.
  • Location Data: We collect precise GPS location data while the App is in use and in the background to provide real-time load tracking, geofence-based arrival detection at pickup and delivery locations, and route monitoring. Location tracking is only active when you are assigned to an active load.
  • Camera and Photos: We access your device camera to capture Bill of Lading (BOL) photos, Proof of Delivery (POD) photos, delivery photos, and electronic signatures. These photos are uploaded and stored securely.
  • Push Notification Token: We collect your device's push notification token to send you load assignments, geofence arrival alerts, and delivery reminders.
  • Device Information: We collect your device platform (iOS/Android) for notification delivery purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To assign and manage freight loads
  • To track delivery progress and provide real-time location updates to shippers and dispatchers
  • To detect arrival at pickup and delivery locations using geofencing
  • To capture and store delivery documentation (BOL, POD, signatures)
  • To send push notifications about load assignments and status updates
  • To verify driver identity and manage account access

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with enterprise-grade security. Photos and documents are stored in encrypted cloud storage. We use row-level security policies to ensure drivers can only access their own data.

Data Sharing

We share your location data and delivery documentation with:

  • Shippers: Real-time location tracking and proof of delivery for their loads
  • Dispatchers: Load management and driver coordination

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Location Data

The App collects location data in the background to enable geofence-based arrival detection at pickup and delivery locations. You can disable location permissions in your device settings at any time, though this will prevent arrival detection and live tracking features from functioning.

Camera Access

The App requests camera access solely for capturing delivery-related photos (BOL, POD, delivery photos) and electronic signatures. Camera access is only used when you actively choose to take a photo or capture a signature within the App.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Disable location or camera permissions through your device settings
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for legal and business purposes. Delivery documentation is retained in accordance with federal freight brokerage record-keeping requirements.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by other means.
